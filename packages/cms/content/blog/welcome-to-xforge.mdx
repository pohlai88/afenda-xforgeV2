## Why file-based content

Marketing sites do not need a proprietary CMS token to ship blog posts and legal pages. MDX files in git give you version control, previews in PRs, and zero runtime API dependency.

## What you get

- Typed frontmatter validated with Zod
- MDX body compiled at build/request time
- Table of contents and reading time generated automatically

## Next steps

Add new posts under `packages/cms/content/blog/` and legal pages under `packages/cms/content/legal/`.

```ts
const post = await blog.getPost("welcome-to-xforge");
```
